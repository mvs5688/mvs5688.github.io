Making chocolate cookies is an easy, fun, and a quick treat. It makes a great after-school snack for kids and a tasty dessert! For this recipe, you will only need 10 ingredients. Now, let's get started!

    Ingredients:
  1. 1 cup of softened butter
  2. 1 cup of white suger
  3. 1 cup of packed brown sugar
  4. 2 eggs
  5. 2 teaspons of vanilla extract
  6. 3 cups of all purpose flour
  7. 1 teaspoon of baking soda
  8. 2 teasoons of hot water
  9. 1/2 a teaspoon of salt
  10. 2 cups of sesime sweet chocotate
*adding a cup of chopped walnuts is optional

Okay. So now that we have the ingridients, we can start baking. This should take about 1 hour. Let's begin!

You're going to start by preheating the oven to 350 degrees F. While that's happening, combine the butter, white sugar, and brown sugar. You then beat the eggs one at a time and add the vanilla. Then dissolve the baking soda in the hot water and add it to the batter along with the salt. Finally stir in the flour and chocolate chips (and walnuts if wished). Ftaer that, take large spoonfulls of the batter and place them on ungreased pans. You will then place that in the pre-heated oven for 10 minutes (or at least unt it's edges turn a shade of light brown.

Now you have completed making these delicios chocolate chip cookies! Enjoy!
